Death Road

Death Road

Death Road is a solo Top Down Shooter game. Take on the challenge of liberating the earth from a zombies and monsters infestation, using over-the-top weaponry in a world with awesomely stylized graphics. The road journey has never been so much fun!

Weapon Category - Machine Gun, Rockets, Shot-Gun, SMG, Sniper, Fireball, Gatling gun, Ice gun, Bolt gun and Of course Flamethrower.


Story

Story - Our world is attacked by Zombies and Monsters. Other soldier troops tried to clean the area but no one survived. A soldier stands to take the responsibility to fight against all zombies and monsters and world hope is relied on him.


Mission

  • One Soldier to Save the World, so Kill one - Kill All, No Mercy, No Pity, No Regret.
  • As you play you can unlock the awesome Achievements.

Tic Tac Toe - Jungle

Tic Tac Toe - Jungle

Tic-Tac-Toe or noughts and crosses, or Xs and Os is a paper-and-pencil game which now we can play on our Android Device for two players, X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3×3 grid. The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row is the winner.


Features

  • Replace old school style Xs and 0s with Jungle Theme
  • Play with friends using offline mode.
  • Play with Android device
  • Play with anyone using online mode.


Leaderboard and Achievements

  • Play and Dominate on the LeaderBoards by achieveing high score and prove your gaming skills to the world.
  • As you play you can unlock the awesome Achievements.

Love On Top

About Game

Love On Top is funtastically colorful and addicting new game that is sure to keep you glued to your phone. To Play pick from six unique characters and tap the screen to start jumping. Tilt the phone left and right from to Jump from one platform to another. Along the way you can pick up the cupid boosters which help you to move even faster. Be sure to avoid monster Birds and look out for moving platforms in order to stay on steady course and rack up a super high score. Simply to play and stylish animated "Love On Top" has all the best aspects of jumping and dodging your way to victory. Start your adventure today.

FREELANCE GAME ARTIST

THE IDEAL CANDIDATE:

We are looking for Game Artist join our team. Your main focus will be on developing new beloved characters for the game. You will need to be a generalist in the sense that you should be able to independently design, execute and make your assets work in Game. You will be surrounded by people who will help you become a better game developer each day, inspire you and push you to break mobile games as we know them today. And you will also do the same to the people you are working with.


RESPONSIBILITIES:

  • Works on the development of a visual style, design and overall look of Game

  • Creates beautiful characters, assets and environments to be rolled out into the game

  • Develops visual concepts and ideas

  • Contributes with artwork ideas toward communities and forums

  • Gets involved in the look and feel of further projects including animation, community and marketing

  • Supports team in game design, working closely with programmers, community and everyone else on a tightly knit game team

  • Generally just nails 2D, 3D and/or UI art for mobile games



REQUIREMENTS:

  • Capability to independently create concept sketches and maybe even final renderings/tweaks and animation

  • Ability to create a variety of production quality, Game style, 3D art

  • Proven experience with 3D software e.g. Maya / 3DS Max

  • Experience with Adobe Photoshop and Flash

  • Pride and responsibility in your work when given the freedom to work independently

  • Modesty and respect when receiving feedback from your team

  • Appreciation and understanding of User Interface design

  • Previous experience in game development and game art
