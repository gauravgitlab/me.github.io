more about me

Gaurav Garg : Born in Delhi, India on June 28th 1986. I was 9 year old when one day my computer teacher was playing game, I forget the name of that game but it was like Mario. Initially i was not interested . Games were just wastage of time for me because i was just a viewer who is looking to others to play. i spend all my free time with comics.

My Enthusiasm was on peek when i was 12 and i went to a gaming parlour with my friend. There i played many games, it was one of the wonderful experience in my life.

As my nature or personality i always try to do something new. While graduating i decided to build my carrier in Gaming industry. I researched a lot and decide to become a programmer instead of animator or designer.

After lots of coding, i finally got my first job in Isis Design Service as a Game Programmer. Here i learnt a lot more than i have learned in my life, before i started working professionally. Now , i already have 5 apps on Apple Store and 1 web based game. But my journey is still so long, i have to achieve lots of target and makes lots of games for other consoles.
